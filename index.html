Our Story

We opened in May 2010, and our cakes has been loved in the neighborhood. We use only selected ingredients and be commited to trustworthy products.

Croissants are our flagship product, which made from french butter, freshly milled organic flours, and long fermentation periods.

To serve a more delightfull experience to our customers, we opened a resturant next to bakery in july 2018. our meals are inspired by the seasons and use freshly fruits and vegetables from the farmers' market.
